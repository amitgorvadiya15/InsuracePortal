What does landlord insurance cover?

Question 1 of 4

As well as buildings and contents insurance, specific landlord policies can cover risks such as tenants defaulting on their rent, loss of rent due to property being damaged and becoming uninhabitable, and cover if someone makes a compensation claim for injury or damage.
